ACHIEVEMENTS

I have recently graduated from Ironhack's Web Development Bootcamp (an intensive 12-weeks course). This Bootcamp was also organized by McKinsey Social Initiative. I learned about HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Ruby and Ruby on Rails, and improved my skills as a developer.

During the last 4 weeks, I developed a Web based project called "Keep Running" with Ruby on Rails. At the end of the Bootcamp, all the students presented their projects. I have been awarded "Best project" due to my effort throughout the bootcamp.


"Keep Running" was created in one month and launched in June 2015.

This is an web application targeted to runners, experts and beginners. The web app lets you choose among different running paths in several cities. Once you have selected a path, the app shows the route details and information such as terrain elevation and weather. "Keep Running" is also a gamified app to keep runners motivated: different experience levels, increased when the user marks running paths as done, rankings of users, and different leagues in which you could be promoted depending on your experience level.
